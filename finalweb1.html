Tsunamis Worldwide

This site shows visualisations representing infromation about tsunamis that have happened over the last 10 years.

Tsunami Locations on a World Map

The visualisation to the right is a map of the world showing the locations where tsunamis have been from 2010 to 2020. The size of the dot represents the magnitude of the earthquake that caused the tsunami. Hovering your mouse over a dot will show you all key information about the tsunmai.

Depth and Magnitude of the Earthquakes That Caused the Tsunamis

This interactive scatterplot visualisation goes into more detail about the earthquakes that caused the tsunamis. It shows you the earthquake depth and magnitude. It is coloured by the region and this can also be used to filter the data. Alike the first visulation a tooltip has been used with key information. .

Density per Year

This interactive area chart shows the number of tsunamis per year in any given region. It may also be filtered using the regions in the legend.

Extent of the Damages per Month

This final visualisation is a stacked bar chart used to show the number of tsunamis per month and the extent of the damage cause by the tsunami. The visualisation can be filtered using the extent of damage in the lengend.

This visualisation is created by Samantha Elliott . The datasource is https://www.kaggle.com/andrewmvd/tsunami-dataset.
